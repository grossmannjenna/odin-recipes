Protein Brownie Batter

This protein brownie batter is the perfect cure for a sweet tooth or late night craving. It fulfills the sweet craving while also bringing you closer to reaching your protein goals. I've even ate this for breakfast in a pinch because of its simplicity and quickness.

I've found that the protein powder you use makes or breaks this recipe so try to use a chocolate protein powder that either does not have a strong taste(this is how mine is) or has a rich chocalatey flavor you enjoy. I have also used water in place of milk, and it can be done but the flavor isn't as rich. Dairy free milks work the same as regular milk, so no need to worry.

Protein:20-25g(depends on your protein powder)

Ingredients

  • Chocolate protein powder
  • Milk
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • salt
  • chocolate chips(optional)
  • chocolate granola(optional)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, add one scoop of protein powder.
  2. Add a small amount of milk, it should be a little more than the size of the protein powder.
  3. Add one tablespoon of the unsweetened cocoa powder.
  4. Stir the milk and powders. The consistency should be like brownie batter, thick and creamy. If you need, add more milk or cocoa powder.
  5. Add a pinch of salt and granola or chocolate chips if desired.
  6. Put in the fridge for at least 20 minutes for the best flavor and consistency.
Enjoy!
