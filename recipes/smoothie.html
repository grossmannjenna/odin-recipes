Protein Smoothie

This protein smoothie is perfect for a quick meal or snack to fuel up before or after a workout or enjoy as a dessert. It can be tailored to your likings and only requires 5 minutes of your time. I love making this smoothie and taking it with me on the go for an easy protein meal.

Protein: 30g(depends on your protein powder and milk)

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Milk
  • Frozen fruit
  • Peanut Butter or PB2(optional)

Steps

  1. Add 8oz of milk to blender
  2. Add about one cup of frozen fruit
  3. Add protein powder and 1 tbsp of penaut butter if desired
  4. Blend
Enjoy!
